About Us

Breaking the Silence

A student-led movement turning stories into strength — building a kinder, safer world, one voice at a time.

We make sure every voice
is heard & valued

Breaking the Silence is a student-led initiative dedicated to raising awareness about bullying, empowering young voices, and creating lasting change in schools and communities around the world. We believe every child deserves to feel safe, respected, and heard.

Poem Rules & Guidelines

  • Open to all students worldwide, any age.
  • Poem must relate to bullying — as a victim, bystander, or advocate for kindness.
  • Maximum 30 lines. Any style welcome (free verse, rhyming, haiku, etc.).
  • Must be your own original work — no AI-generated poems.
  • No offensive language or hate speech.
  • You may submit anonymously — your name won't be shown publicly.
  • Selected poems will be featured in our e-magazine.
  • By submitting you agree your poem may be published on our platform.

Cover Competition Rules

  • Open to all students worldwide, any age.
  • Design must reflect the theme "Breaking the Silence" — anti-bullying.
  • Accepted formats: JPG, PNG, PDF. Max file size: 50 MB.
  • Must be portrait orientation (like a magazine cover).
  • Must be your own original artwork — no AI-generated images.
  • One submission per student.
  • The winning design becomes the official e-magazine cover.
  • By submitting you agree your design may be published on our platform.
